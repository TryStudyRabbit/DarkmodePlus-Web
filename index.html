Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DarkMode+ and its features.

Will DarkMode+ work on all websites?

Yes, DarkMode+ is designed to work on virtually any website. Our smart algorithms apply dark mode styling while preserving images, videos, and other media elements. If you encounter a website where the extension doesn't work optimally, you can easily add it to your exclusion list or try a different theme.

How do I customize the colors in my dark mode?

To customize colors, select "Custom" in the theme dropdown menu in the extension popup. This will open a color customization interface where you can adjust background, text, link, and accent colors. You can use the color pickers or enter specific hex values for precise control.

Will using dark mode save battery on my device?

If your device has an OLED or AMOLED display (common in many smartphones and high-end laptops), using dark mode can significantly reduce power consumption since these displays don't use power to show black pixels. For LCD displays, the power savings are minimal, but you'll still benefit from reduced eye strain.

How do I exclude certain websites from dark mode?

There are two ways to exclude websites: 1) Click the DarkMode+ icon in your browser toolbar while on the website you want to exclude, then click the "Add to exclusion list" button. 2) Go to the extension options page by right-clicking the extension icon and selecting "Options", then add the website domain to your exclusion list manually.

Does DarkMode+ collect any personal data?

No, DarkMode+ does not collect any personal data or browsing history. Your settings are stored locally on your device and can optionally sync across your Chrome browsers using Chrome's built-in sync feature. We respect your privacy and do not track your browsing activity.

Will my settings sync across multiple devices?

Yes, all your DarkMode+ settings, including theme preferences, custom colors, and excluded websites, will automatically sync across all your devices where you're signed into Chrome with the same Google account and have Chrome Sync enabled.
