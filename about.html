About Us

Learn about our story, mission, and the people behind DarkMode+

Our Story

DarkMode+ was born from a real need. In 2023, a small team of developers and designers noticed how much time we were all spending in front of screens, often late into the night. The harsh bright light from standard websites was causing eye strain, disrupting sleep patterns, and making browsing uncomfortable.

We tried various existing dark mode solutions but found them lacking in customization, reliability, and ease of use. Some websites would break, others would have poor color contrast, and most didn't allow for personalization.

So we set out to create a better solution: a dark mode extension that would work flawlessly on any website while giving users complete control over their viewing experience. After months of development and testing, DarkMode+ was launched, quickly becoming a favorite tool for night-time browsers, developers, and anyone looking to reduce eye strain.

Today, DarkMode+ continues to evolve with regular updates and new features based on feedback from our growing community of users.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us and the principles that guide our work

Our Mission

At DarkMode+, our mission is to make digital experiences more comfortable, accessible, and personalized. We believe that technology should adapt to human needs, not the other way around.

We're committed to:

  • Reducing digital eye strain and its impact on health and wellbeing
  • Improving accessibility for photosensitive users
  • Empowering users with control over their browsing experience
  • Creating technology that respects user privacy and operates transparently

Through continuous innovation and listening to our users, we aim to make DarkMode+ the most effective and customizable dark mode solution available.

Our Values

  • User-Centered Design

    Every feature and improvement is developed with our users' needs in mind. We regularly gather feedback and prioritize enhancements that make the most difference.

  • Quality & Reliability

    We're committed to delivering a product that works consistently and reliably across all websites, with thorough testing and continuous improvement.

  • Respect for Privacy

    We believe in transparency and respect for user data. DarkMode+ operates entirely on your device, with no tracking or collection of personal information.

  • Continuous Innovation

    We're always exploring new ways to improve the dark mode experience, staying ahead of web technologies and evolving user needs.
